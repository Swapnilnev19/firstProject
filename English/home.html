Brief History

Shree Devi Chamundeshwari Temple in Andurle is believed to be established approximately six hundred years ago by Patil Patkar families originally hailing from Pat and surrounding villages. Patil families were Gramadhipati (village-in-charge) of Pat and surrounding villages. By 1925, the old temple was in dire status with idol having had developed cracks with passage of time.During the visit by Late Shree Krishnaji Shivaji Patkar and Shree Ramchandra Krishnaji Patkar in 1927…..
