The vidhi (process) will be presided over by Purohit of the temple and wherever required, assistance from additional Brahmins will be sought by Purohit post obtaining prior concurrence from devotee.

For performing Navachandi Poojaduring the annual vardhapandinfestival or any other day, please contact Committee or Purohitof the temple well in advance.

Important instructions:

Committee has installed a mini-idol of Shree Devi in front of main idol to perform day to day Poojaand other rituals. All are requested to avoid touching of mainidol to keep sanctity, purityand inviolability of the same and protect it from wear and tear as well as from impact of flowers, turmeric, vermilion, Kumkum, sandalwood paste and other Poojamaterial.

Shree Kul-Purusha bhakti

The white platform (altar) with image of feet of Kul Purusha represents Mul-Purusha (original person from whom Patil Patkar family originated.

Type of service Charges (In Rupees)
Pavamana Abhishek To be updated
Rudra Abhishek To be updated
PurushaSukta Abhishek To be updated
Laghu Rudra To be updated
Rudra Ekadashami To be updated

The committee reserves right for timely revision in charges. All the devotees are requested to contact Purohit/Manager at Andurle and confirm for the same. The expenses in respect of Navachandi / Saptashati Puja will be informed by Purohit.

No religious activities other than mentioned above are performed in the temple.

Any devotee intending to offer saree or similar precious garment (cloth) to Devi is required to submit same to Purohit or representatives of local committee in Temple premises and get an acknowledgement/receipt.
