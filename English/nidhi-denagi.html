Funds & Donations

Shree Devi Chamundeshwari Sewa Samiti is registered under Societies Registration Act 1860 and Mumbai Public Trust Registration Act 1950.

The donation can be made through cheque drawn in favour of “Shree Devi Chamundeshwari Sewa Samiti”. To directly deposit funds in Samiti bank account through electronic mode, please note following details.

Post donation, please inform following to the committee on e-mail.

  • Name of the donor
  • Address (with pincode)
  • Purpose of donation
  • Gotra
  • Amount donated*
  • Date of donation
  • UTR No. / Transaction reference no.
  • Remitters Bank name and address
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Mobile
  • Landline (if available)

In case of donation by cheque, please send same with above details (except point 6 and 7, to the address of the committee in Mumbai or to Purohit / Manager in Andurle.

The committee accepts donations for following funds:

Nitya Puja Nidhi

The devotees can perform puja on a particular pre-fixed date of every year on behalf of self or in the name of any related individual (e.g., Son/Daughter/parents/other relatives or friends). The date is accepted based on Roman calendar. Every year on said date, a puja will be performed and vibhuti/Prasad will be dispatched to such person on the address provided. One needs to provide following information to committee at the time of registering of Nitya Puja.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Gotra
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Mobile
  • Landline (if available)

The sum required to be deposited in Nitya Puja Nidhi for each Puja is Rs.750/- one time. The committee reserves right for timely revision in charges.

List of Nitya Pooja at present with committee based on available records.

If any of the devotee intends to change his old address with new address for Nitya Puja, please write to Sewa Samiti with details of serial no., date, name and old as well as new address and contact no./e-mail. In case the unfortunate demise of the person in whose name the Nitya Puja is conducted, please do inform to Sewa Samiti.

Nandadeep Nidhi

The funds are used to purchase oil for continuous burning of hanging brass lamp (Lamandiva) in front of Shree Devi. The data to be provided with each request for donating in this fund will be as follows.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Mobile
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Landline (if available)
Mandir Premises Jirnoddhar Nidhi

The fund was set up to bear cost to carry out repairs to temple from time to time. The data to be provided with each request for donating the amount in this fund is as follows.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Mobile
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Landline (if available)
Mandir Premises Maintenance Nidhi

The fund is used to maintain the temple premises including painting, decoration during festivals, cleaning, lighting, watering of trees in temple courtyard, waterproofing, and maintenance of furniture and fittings.The data to be provided with each request for donating the funds will be as follows.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Mobile
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Landline (if available)
Devi Utsav Nidhi

The fund is used to celebrate the two days annual Utsav when a Navachandi Puja is conducted. It also covers expenses for daily puja. The same is conducted every year on Magh Shukla Panchami and Magh Shukla Shashti. A Prasad (holy food) is distributed to devotees on both the days.

The data to be provided with each request for donating in this fund is as follows.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Mobile
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Landline (if available)
Bhakta Niwas construction Nidhi

The fund is used to improve and renovate as well as carry out repairs to Bhakta Niwas built to facilitate stay of devotees during visit to Devi Temple. A donation to said fund will help committee to enlarge and improve infrastructure for servicing of devotees. The data to be provided with each request for donating the funds will be as follows.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Mobile
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Landline (if available)
Navaratra Utsav Nidhi

Navaratra Utsav Nidhi

The fund is used to bear cost of religious rituals during nine days of Ashwin Navaratra. During the Navaratra, puja vidhi are performed in temple as desired by devotees and temple is decorated.

During the Navaratra, committee arranges cultural events such as Garba, sports competition and other events to encourage local talent and provides a platform to budding artists to present their skills.

The data to be provided with each request for donating the funds will be as follows.

  • Name of the donor
  • Full postal address with pin code
  • Date (as per Roman Calendar) when the Puja is required to be conducted each year
  • Mobile
  • E-mail (if available)
  • Landline (if available)

Donations in Kind

If any devotee intends to donate Gold/Silver/other, ornaments or valuable utensils or any other metal or other non-perishable items of value, he/she should contact committee in advance and provide intimation of his/her intentions. This will help committee with sufficient time to take such valuable items on record, account them and deposit items in safe custody.

The committee reserves right to accept ornament donations, subject to the assessment of its value, weight and authenticity; and obtain declaration from donor in appropriate form.

Those devotees who intend to deposit small amount of cash in currency notes or coins, may deposit same in donation box installed near door of the garbhagriha.
